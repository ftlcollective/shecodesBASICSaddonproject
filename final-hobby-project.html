Design Concepts

The Future of Wearable Prints

Concept 01

Bio Responsive Lattice Gown

A flexible TPU structure that reacts to body heat and movement. Embedded chromic inks shift colour across the lattice as the wearer moves, making every walk down the runway a unique display.

Concept 02

Exo Armour Shoulders

Carbon fibre infused PLA shoulder pieces inspired by insect exoskeletons. Structural geometry that is both protective and sculptural.

Concept 03

Voronoi Sole Footwear

Biomimetic footwear with a Voronoi lattice midsole. Engineered for weight distribution and spring energy return. No two soles are identical.

Concept 04

Programmable Smart Textile

Conductive filament woven into a matrix of addressable LED nodes. Each intersection can be programmed via Bluetooth to display patterns, react to music, or change based on environment data. Fashion becomes a wearable display.

Concept 05

AR Integrated Face Visor

A printed face shield housing AR projection lenses. Circuit traces printed in conductive PLA link sensors directly to the wearable processor built into the collar.

Concept 06

Parametric Resin Jewellery

High resolution SLA printed jewellery with algorithm generated geometries. Each piece is unique, generated from a mathematical seed and cast in translucent photopolymer resin.
